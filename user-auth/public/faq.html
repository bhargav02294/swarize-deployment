Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Swarize?

Swarize is a free-to-sell e-commerce platform exclusively for India, offering engaged traffic and advertising support.

Who can sell on Swarize?

Anyone in India can sell on Swarize. Whether you're an individual, a small business, or a brand, you can start selling with zero registration fees.

How does the Swarize promo code work?

After every successful purchase on Swarize, you will receive a promo code via email that can be used on your next order. This promo code gives you a 5% discount on the product price. It is applicable for one-time use only. This is our way of rewarding loyal buyers and helping you save more on future purchases!

For Sellers

How to register as a seller?

Sign up, complete your profile, and start listing products. No fees needed.

Is GST required for selling?

If turnover is above ₹40 lakh (₹20 lakh in some states), GST is needed. Otherwise, you can sell without GST.

How to add products?

Go to "Add Product," fill details, upload images, and set price.

Is there a limit to how many products I can list?

No, you can add unlimited products for free.

Who handles shipping?

Swarize handles shipping for you. Shipping charges apply.

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

No, COD is not available.

Can buyers return products?

Return policy depends on the seller. Swarize does not have a return policy.

When will I receive my payments?

Payments are credited in T+4 working days after successful order completion.

For Buyers

How to place an order?

Browse, add to cart, and checkout.

Payment options available?

UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, wallets. No COD.

How do I get a promo code?

When you purchase a product on Swarize, you will receive a promo code via email after the order is successfully placed.

How does the promo code benefit me as a buyer?

The promo code lets you enjoy a 5% discount on your next purchase, making shopping on Swarize more rewarding.

Will I get another promo code if I use the first one?

Yes! Every time you complete a purchase, you will receive a new promo code for your next order.

Any hidden charges?

No, the displayed price is final.

How to track orders?

Use the "Collections" page with your order ID.

What if my product is damaged?

Contact Swarize support immediately.

Policies

Where can I read the full terms and conditions?

You can view our Terms & Conditions page for all policies, including transaction fees, seller responsibilities, and platform usage.

How is Swarize different from Amazon & Flipkart?

Swarize is a reselling and dropshipping-friendly e-commerce platform that makes it easy for anyone to start and grow their online business without investing in inventory. Unlike Amazon and Flipkart, which primarily focus on direct sellers and brands, Swarize is designed to empower resellers, dropshippers, and small businesses with minimal risk and maximum profitability.

Are there any monthly charges?

No monthly charges, only a small transaction fee per sale.

Can I sell internationally?

No, Swarize is for Indian sellers and buyers only.

Can I negotiate prices with the seller?

No, prices are fixed by the seller, but sellers may offer discounts during promotions.

Can I buy digital products on Swarize?

No, Swarize only supports physical product sales.

Is there a mobile app for Swarize?

Currently, Swarize is accessible via a mobile-friendly website. A mobile app may be introduced in the future.
