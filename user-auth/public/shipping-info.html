Your Guide to Smooth and Timely Deliveries with Swarize

Two Shipping Methods

At Swarize, sellers have the flexibility to choose between two shipping options:

  • 1. Seller Ships Directly: The seller handles packaging and shipping. The estimated delivery time is faster since the seller ships the product directly.
  • 2. Swarize Fulfillment: If the seller does not have the product in stock, Swarize sources it from other suppliers and handles the shipping. This may take slightly longer but ensures product availability.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email and SMS with a tracking ID. You can track your order using:

  • Website: Go to "Collections" in your Swarize account and check
  • SMS & Email: Tracking details will be sent upon dispatch.

4. Delivery Policies

  • Dispatch Time: Sellers ship orders within 24-48 hours of order confirmation.
  • Address Accuracy: Ensure correct shipping details to prevent delays.
  • Signature Requirement: Some orders may require a signature upon delivery.
  • Note: Swarize does not support Cash on Delivery (COD). All orders must be prepaid.

5. Lost, Delayed, or Damaged Shipments

For delayed, lost, or damaged orders:

  • Contact support if your order is delayed beyond the estimated time.
  • Report lost or damaged shipments within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Swarize is not responsible for delays due to natural disasters, strikes, or logistical issues.

6. Returns & Refunds

At Swarize, we currently do not support Return to Origin (RTO). This means:

  • Once an order is shipped, it cannot be returned to the seller due to non-delivery or refusal by the buyer.
  • Buyers must ensure they provide the correct shipping address and contact details to avoid failed deliveries.
