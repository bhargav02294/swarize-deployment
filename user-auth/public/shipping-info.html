3. Order Tracking

Track your shipment easily:

  • Website: Go to your account → Collections → Order Tracking
  • SMS/Email: Tracking ID sent automatically once shipped

4. Delivery Policies

  • Sellers dispatch within 24–48 hours.
  • Ensure correct address to avoid delays.
  • Signature may be required at delivery.
  • COD is not supported — prepaid only.

5. Lost, Delayed, or Damaged Shipments

If your order is delayed, lost, or damaged:

  • Contact us if delivery exceeds estimated time.
  • Report damaged/lost items within 48 hours.
  • Delays due to weather/strikes/logistics are handled case-by-case.

6. Returns & Refunds

Swarize does not support Return-to-Origin (RTO):

  • Orders cannot be returned once shipped.
  • Buyers must provide accurate address & details.
